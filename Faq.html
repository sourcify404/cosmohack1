Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about CosmoHack

What is CosmoHack?

▼
CosmoHack is a 46-hour virtual hackathon organized by the Sourcify community, bringing together coders, space enthusiasts, and innovators to solve real-world challenges with emerging technologies. The event takes place from March 22-24, 2025.

Who can participate?

▼
CosmoHack is open to participants of all skill levels, from beginners to experienced developers. We welcome students, professionals, and space enthusiasts from around the world.

What kind of projects can I work on?

▼
Projects can range from software solutions for space missions to innovative applications of space data. Some examples include AI for space debris tracking, VR experiences for astronaut training, and apps for citizen science in astronomy.

When does registration close?

▼
Registration is currently open and will close on March 2nd, 2025. Don't miss out on this amazing opportunity to showcase your skills and connect with like-minded innovators!

Is there a registration fee?

▼
No, participation in CosmoHack is completely free. We believe in making space innovation accessible to everyone.

What are the prizes?

▼
Prizes include cash awards, internship opportunities with leading space companies, and the chance to present your project to space industry experts. Full details will be announced closer to the event date.
