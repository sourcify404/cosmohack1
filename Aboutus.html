WHAT IS COSMOHACK

COSMOHACK is a dynamic Virtual Online hackathon organized by the Sourcify community, bringing together talented individuals to solve real-world challenges with emerging technologies. This 24-hour coding marathon will take place from November 19-20, 2025, featuring exciting tracks, amazing prizes, and opportunities to connect with industry leaders. Join us in inspiring change and driving meaningful impact through innovation.

Cosmohack is designed to:

  • Empower students in technology
  • Foster innovation and creativity
  • Provide a supportive learning environment
  • Connect emerging talent with industry leaders
